Our Vision of Elite Security

Private security officers are part of the private sector, work for private companies, and are paid by an employer. Police officers are part of the public sector, work for the town or state, and are paid by the state.

Our vision is to bridge the gap between Police Officer and Private Security Officer by hiring Prior Police, and military Police officials. This brings a new look to the private security sector. This allows other security officers that have not had ploice experience work with ones who have.

Our security officer remain active during their shifts. We randomize our foot, and mobile patrols to maintain the protection of the client property.

Situational Awareness at is finest

Situational awareness is the perception of environmental elements and events with respect to time or space, the comprehension of their meaning, and the projection of their future status.

Along with the exstensive trainign and briefing in situational awareness, Our officers must have a thorough understanding of situational understanding. This type training gives officers the ability to apply analysis and judgment to the companies situation awareness to determine the relationships of the factors present and form logical conclusions concerning threats to the force or mission accomplishment, opportunities for mission accomplishment, and gaps in information".
